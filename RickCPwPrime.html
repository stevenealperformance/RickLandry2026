Three methods, different assumptions. The 3-parameter Morton fit (best R²) is the reference. Intervals.icu's ExPM distributes short-duration work differently, which is why its eW′ number reads much larger.

Interpretation

CP agreement is tight — three independent methods land within 239–263 W, with the high-confidence Morton fit at 245 W (3.14 W/kg). W′ is less settled: the classical "energy above CP" figure is ~8–12 kJ, while intervals.icu's 20 kJ reflects a model that bundles short-duration anaerobic contribution differently. Use 8–12 kJ for classical pacing calculations (W′bal), 20 kJ only inside intervals.icu's ecosystem.
